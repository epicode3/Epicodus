About

We at Epicodus (A research and education-based startup ), are a team with an aim to provide an interactive learning platform for students to learn,research and develop projects with our support. We strive to cultivate a change in the current learning strategy of students and incorporate a reasonable learning methodology, to make them future-prepared with good knowledge of technologies and profound practical experience in those fields with developing projects.
We believe that you will appreciate this learning excursion and shape your future with us at Epicodus.
